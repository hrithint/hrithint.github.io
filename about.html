About Navculus

Discover the story behind our journey to create comprehensive naval architecture calculation tools that comply with international rules and regulations.

Hrithin Thavorath

Naval Architect | Kochi

I am a naval architect from Kochi, India, who has been developing Navculus since 2022. Like many early-career professionals, I faced challenges in building my career in naval architecture.

At the initial stage of my career, I tried to build a new application for naval architecture calculations, but my lack of knowledge in coding made it extremely difficult for me.

However, I didn't let this obstacle stop me. After three years of continuous studies and self-development in both naval architecture and programming, I successfully developed an application called Navculus for ship-related calculations.

Initially, I created a mobile application, but I realized that naval architects often work on desktops where mobile apps aren't as accessible. This realization led me to start developing web-based solutions, culminating in the creation of navculus.in - one of my long-held dreams.

Navculus provides various calculation modules that comply with respective rules and regulations, making complex naval architecture computations more accessible to professionals in the field.

I hope everyone finds the interface intuitive and helpful for their work. Thank you for your support as I continue to improve and expand Navculus.
